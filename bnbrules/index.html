Welcome to bits & Bites!

To make sure you have a great time here, we'll need to lay down some rules... just so we make sure people don't go too far.

How the rules work

  1. If you break a rule, you may be given an official warning from a member of the staff.
  2. Once you have received enough warnings, you will be placed in jail, where you must explain why you have broken the rules so many times.
  3. If, after being placed in jail, your behaviour has not been rectified, you will be considered for a ban.
  • Rules are based on intent. If we haven't specifically mentioned something in a rule, but the intent is obviously to stop you from doing said thing, don't do it.
  • A moderator may skip any of the above steps in exceptional circumstances.

Official Rules

The official bits & Bytes rules are laid out below.

Tier 1 offences

Tier 1 offences incur an unofficial warning from a member of staff. However, if it is repetitive, then an official warning may be given.

  • Keep conversation flowing in all channels, except for #off-topic.
  • Don't spam the chat. This includes @mention spam.
  • Don't be a backseat moderator.
  • Keep conversation flowing in all channels, except for #off-topic.
  • A mod's decision is final. If you feel that you must escalate the issue, ask an admin.
  • Don't spam the reactions. This includes many reactions on one message and one reaction on many messages.
  • Keep conversation flowing in all channels, except for #off-topic. I can't stress how important this rule is.

Tier 2 offences

Tier 2 offences incur an official warning from a member of staff.

  • Don't @mention staff unless you need permission now.
  • Content must be kept SFW.
  • Advertising is forbidden on the server.

Tier 3 offences

Tier 3 offences incur an immediate jail or ban, based on the discretion of the staff member administering the punishment.

  • Impersonation is prohibited.
  • Only one account may be made on this server at any time.
  • Malicious content may not be placed in any way on the server.

Also, the Discord Community Guidelines are a good read. Also, make sure you abide by the Discord Terms of Service.

Nickname Policy

In regards to nicknames, we have a policy in place so that your name does not give you an unfair advantage in the Weekly Chat, and so conversations can be undertaken normally. Here are the requirements:

  1. Nicknames must be mentionable and not attention grabbing.
  2. Nicknames must not be used to get on top of the list.
  3. Nicknames must not be too long. Generally, if Discord has to truncate the nickname, that's pushing it.
  4. Nicknames must not contain anything offensive.
  5. Nicknames must not be changed too often. There is a 1 day cooldown on AstralMod to regulate this.

And finally, use common sense. If you have to ask someone if what you're doing is ok, it probably isn't.
