CERIUM

Cerium is a fun little Discord bot created by projsh_

COMMANDS

Command Description
about Displays information about the bot.
avatar Displays your avatar or another user's avatar.
flip Flips a coin for you!
help Displays the help message.
host Displays information about the host's computer.
nick Changes your nickname.
ping Replies with a message.
rtime Displays the bot's response time.
ship Sends you on a ship with the love of your life! (or rather someone randomly picked on the server).
sinfo Displays information about the server.
uinfo Displays information about your account or another user's account.
uptime Displays how long Cerium has been online without restarting.

MUSIC COMMANDS

Command Description
play Plays a video from YouTube in your current voice channel.
skip Skips a video in the queue.
stop Stops the player and clears the queue.
pause Pauses a video.
resume Resumes a video.
